Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Jubilant Breeze ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting user data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information across all applications published and operated by Jubilant Breeze.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 User-Provided Content

Our applications may allow users to upload photos, images, or other content, which may include images containing human faces. This content is voluntarily provided by the user for app functionality.

1.2 Face Data

Some applications may process face data when users upload images containing human faces. Face data refers to visual facial information such as facial features, contours, and structure visible in images.

We do NOT:

  • Perform facial recognition
  • Identify or verify a person’s identity
  • Create or store biometric identifiers
  • Track users across apps using face data

1.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • App usage statistics and interaction data
  • Crash reports and diagnostic logs

2. Use of Face Data

Face data is used only to provide the core functionality requested by the user, including:

  • Generating AI-based images or visual outputs
  • Applying artistic styles, effects, or transformations
  • Producing the final output selected by the user

Face data is not used for identification, authentication, profiling, or advertising purposes.

3. Artificial Intelligence Processing

Some applications operated by Jubilant Breeze use artificial intelligence technologies. AI processing may involve a combination of in-house systems and third-party AI service providers.

  • User content may be transmitted securely for AI processing
  • Processing is performed only to deliver requested outputs
  • User data is not used to train generalized AI models

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We may share limited data with trusted third-party service providers solely to operate and improve our services, including:

  • AI processing and inference services
  • Cloud infrastructure and storage providers
  • Analytics and crash monitoring tools

All third parties are contractually required to protect user data and comply with applicable privacy laws.

Face data is never sold or shared for marketing or advertising.

5. Data Storage and Retention

  • Uploaded content is stored temporarily for processing
  • Face data is deleted automatically after processing or within a short retention window
  • Generated outputs may be stored temporarily for user access

We retain data only for as long as necessary to provide requested services or meet legal requirements.

6. In-App Purchases

Some applications may offer In-App Purchases such as credits or subscriptions. All payments are processed by Apple through the App Store. Jubilant Breeze does not collect or store payment information.

7. Advertising

Some applications may display advertisements from third-party advertising partners. These partners may collect non-personal data such as device identifiers to serve ads.

Uploaded images and face data are never used for advertising purposes.

8. User Rights and Control

Users maintain control over their uploaded content. You may delete generated outputs or discontinue use of our applications at any time.

Temporary processing data is automatically removed from our systems.

9. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect user data, including encryption and secure storage.

10. Children’s Privacy

Our applications are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be reflected with an updated "Last updated" date.
